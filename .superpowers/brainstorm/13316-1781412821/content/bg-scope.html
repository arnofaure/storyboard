Background color — what should it apply to?

You want a White / Grey / Dark grey background option. Two different things this could mean:

A

Canvas / grid area only

The toolbar stays dark (current app theme), but the area behind your storyboard frames switches between white / grey / dark grey — useful for previewing how shots look against different backdrops.

Preview
Toolbar (always dark)
B

Whole app theme

White / grey / dark grey switches the entire app's look — toolbar, page background, card surfaces, text colors all adapt (like a light/dark mode toggle).
